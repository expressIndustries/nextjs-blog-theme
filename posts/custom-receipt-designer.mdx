The original receipt maker [ExpressExpense.com](https://ExpressExpense.com) is often copied by other systems, however competitors have not been able to keep up with their technology.  

[ExpenseFAST.com](https://expensefast.com) is a different kind of receipt maker.  It is more of a receipt design tool.  It is a WYSIWYG type tool that allows you to choose from many receipt fonts and create your receipt from scratch.  There are also many [pre-built templates](https://expensefast.com/receipt-templates/) that allow you to modify those receipt designs and quickly generate receipts for companies like Home Depot, Venmo, Apple store, sneaker companies (StockX, Nike, Adidas and many more), Louis Vuitton, Prada..etc.
